01 About

I'm Hao Tieu Kim (Tieu Kim Hao), a Data Engineer based in Vietnam. I hold a Bachelor of Data Science from the University of Information Technology (VNU-HCM), and I like turning messy, large-scale raw data into clean, reliable datasets that people can actually make decisions on.

Most recently at ConsensysAI I built weekly pipelines processing ~1 million product reviews and shipped LLM-based sentiment & topic extraction with the OpenAI API. Before that, as a DevOps Engineer at OPSWAT, I ran CI/CD, Kubernetes and AWS environments for a malware-detection product. I care about pipelines that are automated, observable and reproducible — and I write long-form guides about data engineering in my blog and essentials.
